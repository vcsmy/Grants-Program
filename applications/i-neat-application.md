## Project Overview :page_facing_up:

### Overview

iNEAT ERP is a comprehensive Enterprise Resource Planning system for cleaning services companies that integrates AI/ML capabilities with Polkadot blockchain technology. The system addresses critical inefficiencies in the cleaning services industry through decentralized verification, automated payments, and transparent service tracking.

**Tagline:** Revolutionizing cleaning services through AI-powered optimization and blockchain verification.

**Brief Description:** iNEAT ERP combines traditional ERP functionality with cutting-edge Web3 technology, creating a scalable framework for service-based industries. The system leverages Polkadot's interoperability features and advanced machine learning capabilities to enable real-time service verification, automated cross-chain payments, and predictive analytics.

**Substrate/Polkadot/Kusama Integration:** We will build a custom Substrate parachain for cleaning service verification and payment processing, implement Polkadot's DID standard for identity management, and use XCM for cross-chain payment processing. The system will integrate with Polkadot.js for wallet connectivity and transaction management.

**Team Interest:** Our team is passionate about bringing practical blockchain applications to traditional industries. We believe that the cleaning services industry, with its complex logistics, quality assurance challenges, and payment disputes, is an ideal candidate for blockchain transformation. The combination of AI/ML optimization and blockchain verification creates a powerful solution for real-world business problems.

### Project Details

**Expected Final State:**
- A fully functional Substrate parachain with specialized pallets for cleaning service management
- AI/ML models integrated with on-chain verification for route optimization and quality prediction

**Technology Stack:**

- **Web3**: Substrate Interface, Web3.py, Polkadot.js integration
- **Mobile**: React Native with JWT authentication
- **Blockchain**: Custom Substrate parachain, XCM, DID standards

**Core Components:**
- Service verification pallets (tracking, quality assessment, payment processing)
- DID implementation using Polkadot's identity framework

- Smart contracts for automated decision-making
- Supply chain tracking with NFT integration
